 SOURCE 1 {
Researchers surveyed nearly 3000 incoming college students and put them in 3 profiles.
Active users (which was 32.4% of the group) Which is defined as people who are active across four different social platforms. they were observed to being at an increased risk of anxiety depression and more stress then the average user.

The second profile would be the passive user (which was 25.3% of the sample group.)used fewer platforms, mainly for entertainment here and there and keeping up with friends and family. They were observed to be less at risk of of falling into depression or anxiety then the active user.

the last profile is going to be the average user which is 42% of the sample population has been shown to meet in the middle of both of the previous in terms of not missing out but not being as high of a risk of depression and anxiety as others
